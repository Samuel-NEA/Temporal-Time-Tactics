Temporal Time Tactics

About

In the year 2046, two academically gifted scientists, Liam and Rose, are trying to invent a new renewable energy source that is very efficient and easy to use. However, something unexpected happens which causes them to create a time machine!


Liam, feeling all-powerful, develops a God complex and wants to use this machine to "fix" the world by any means necessary. This involves killing criminals before they commit crimes and altering the past. He wants to save humanity from destroying itself and he will not give up until he has accomplished his goal. He brings his son, called Simon, along with him.


Rose, who is very moral, disagrees with Liam and thinks that the machine should be destroyed. She thinks that nobody should have that power and that Liam is essentially taking away free will.
